Create View LoginDep
as
Select HD.Name,HE.LoginID from HumanResources.Department HD,HumanResources.Employee HE
left outer join HumanResources.EmployeeDepartmentHistory HED
on HE.BusinessEntityID = HED.BusinessEntityID
Where HD.DepartmentID=HED.DepartmentID
